# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) — EDU-TRACK

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs the use of EDU-TRACK by users of [[LICENSEE_SCHOOL_NAME]] ("School").

## 1. Purpose

EDU-TRACK is provided to support legitimate school operations. Users must use it responsibly, lawfully, and in a manner that protects learners, staff, and the School.

## 2. Prohibited Activities

Users must not:

- access data they are not authorized to view;
- share passwords or use another user’s account;
- upload malware, attempt intrusion, scan/attack systems, or bypass security controls;
- alter grades, fees, attendance, or records without authorization;
- use the system to harass, threaten, or discriminate;
- export or disclose student/staff personal data without authorization;
- use the system for personal commercial activities unrelated to the School.

## 3. Account Security

- Use strong passwords and keep them confidential.
- Report suspected compromise immediately to the School administrator.

## 4. Auditing and Monitoring

The School may monitor usage and maintain logs for security, compliance, and operational reasons.

## 5. Violations

Violations may result in access suspension, disciplinary action, and/or legal action.
