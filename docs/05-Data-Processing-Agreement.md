# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) — EDU-TRACK (Support Access)

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is entered into between:

- **Controller (School):** [[LICENSEE_SCHOOL_NAME]]
- **Processor (Vendor):** EDUTRACK

This DPA applies only to the extent the Vendor processes personal data on behalf of the School, typically when the School authorizes access for technical support.

## 1. Roles

- The School is the **Data Controller**.
- The Vendor is the **Data Processor** only when it accesses/processes School personal data for support.

## 2. Subject Matter and Duration

- **Subject matter:** technical support and troubleshooting for EDU-TRACK.
- **Duration:** for the period Vendor is granted access and/or for the support term (if any).

## 3. Nature and Purpose of Processing

- access to application logs, configuration, and limited records to diagnose issues;
- applying patches, restoring service, or assisting with upgrades.

## 4. Categories of Data Subjects and Personal Data

- **Data subjects:** students, parents/guardians, staff, administrators.
- **Personal data:** identifiers, contact details, academic records, attendance, fee records, and system access logs, depending on what is stored by the School.

## 5. Controller Instructions

Vendor shall process personal data only on documented instructions from the School, including access windows and permitted actions.

## 6. Confidentiality

Vendor personnel with access to personal data shall be bound by confidentiality obligations.

## 7. Security Measures

Vendor will implement reasonable measures appropriate to the support access model, including:

- access control and least privilege;
- secure channels for remote access where used;
- logging of support actions where feasible.

## 8. Subprocessors

Vendor will not appoint subprocessors to process School personal data without prior written authorization from the School, except where required to deliver agreed support tooling [[SUBPROCESSOR_PLACEHOLDER]].

## 9. Data Breach

Vendor will notify the School without undue delay upon becoming aware of a personal data breach relating to Vendor’s processing.

## 10. Return/Deletion

Upon completion of support activities (or termination of support), Vendor will delete any School personal data copied to Vendor systems for support purposes, unless retention is required by law.

## 11. Audit

Upon reasonable notice, the School may request information to confirm Vendor’s compliance with this DPA, subject to confidentiality and security constraints.
