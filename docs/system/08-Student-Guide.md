# EDU-TRACK — Student Guide

This guide is for students using EDU-TRACK to view their profile, academic performance, and fee information (where enabled by the school).

## 1. Getting started

### Step 1: Get your login details

- Request your username/admission number and initial password from the school administrator or class teacher.
- Keep your login details private.

### Step 2: Sign in

- Open the EDU-TRACK web address provided by your school.
- Enter your username/admission number (or email, depending on setup) and password.

### Step 3: If you cannot sign in

- Confirm you typed the correct details (especially admission number).
- If you forgot your password, request a reset from the administrator.

## 2. Update your profile (where allowed)

### Step 1: Open your profile

- Navigate to your profile page.
- Confirm your name, admission number, class/stream, and term.

### Step 2: Report incorrect details

- If any details are wrong, report to the administrator. Students should not change official school records without approval.

## 3. View academic performance

### Step 1: Open results/grades

- Navigate to Results/Performance.
- Select the correct term/semester.

### Step 2: View subject breakdown

- Review marks by subject.
- Confirm the assessment category (CAT/Midterm/Endterm) where applicable.

### Step 3: If results look wrong

- Confirm you selected the correct term.
- Notify your class teacher if a mark appears missing or incorrect.

## 4. View attendance (if enabled)

### Step 1: Open attendance

- Navigate to Attendance.
- Select the date range or term view.

### Step 2: Report attendance issues

- If you believe an attendance record is incorrect, notify your class teacher promptly.

## 5. View fees and statements (if enabled)

### Step 1: Open fee balance

- Navigate to Fees/Finance.
- View your current balance and payment history.

### Step 2: Download/print statement

- Use the statement option (print/download) if available.
- Confirm the statement shows:

  - opening balance
  - charges posted
  - payments received
  - closing balance

### Step 3: If balance looks wrong

- Confirm payments were receipted correctly.
- Report the issue to the finance office with your receipt number/transaction reference.

## 6. Notifications (SMS/email) (optional)

If your school enables notifications:

- Ensure your guardian contact details are correct.
- Do not share sensitive information via SMS.

## 7. Account safety and acceptable use

- Do not share your password.
- Sign out after using shared devices.
- Use the system responsibly according to school ICT policies.
